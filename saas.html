How does Booknetic SaaS work?

Your dashboard

Here you will do all the setup of your business, see the list of registered tenants, payment history, etc. In short, everything related to your business will be managed from this intuitive super-admin panel.

Tenant dashboard

It is a special dashboard reserved for tenants who will be registered from your system. Where your tenants will see and manage everything related to their services.

What is a Planly?

Planly is a social media scheduling tool for TikTok and Instagram.

What is the difference between Planly and other scheduling tools?

We get this question very often. Planly lets you auto publish your posts to TikTok and Instagram. Compared to other scheduling tools, we require no push notifications or manual interaction afterward.
Also, you can schedule Instagram carousel posts, stories, first comments, and pin posts from personal accounts with Planly.

Can I schedule posts to a personal account?

Absolutely. Planly lets you schedule your posts to personal, creator, and business accounts.

Can I use Planly for free?

Of course, you can. You can create a free account at Planly and also use 14-day free trials of our premium plans.
